Fermer X

Komorebi album re-design

The project

I re-designed slenderbodies’ CD cover and booklet for their album Komorebi. Komorebi, in Japanese, roughly translates to the sunlight coming through the branches of trees. The photographs are all mine.

Software used

Adobe Photoshop for the photo editing
Adobe Illustrator for creating the illustrations
Adobe InDesign for the page layout
