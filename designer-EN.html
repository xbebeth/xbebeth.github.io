Komorebi album re-design

The project

I re-designed slenderbodies’ CD cover and booklet for their album Komorebi. Komorebi, in Japanese, roughly translates to the sunlight coming through the branches of trees. The photographs are all mine.

Software used

Adobe Photoshop for the photo editing
Adobe Illustrator for creating the illustrations
Adobe InDesign for the page layout

Abstract typography z fold brochure

The project

I created a brochure illustrating the lyrics to Everything Everything’s song We Sleep in Pairs. I wanted to create something that would visually represent the rhythm of the song and how it contrasts with the apprehension of the words.

Software used

Adobe InDesign for the page layout & typography
Adobe Photoshop for photo editing
Adobe Illustrator for distortion of typography

Lord of the Flies book design

The project

I re-designed the cover for William Golding’s novel Lord of the Flies. I wanted to create an illustration representative of the eeriness I felt when reading the book while also referring to the depth of the woods which the characters venture into.

Software used

Adobe Photoshop for the page layout and typography
